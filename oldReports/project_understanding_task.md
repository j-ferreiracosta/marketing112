**Project Summary: Inovafil Linen Yarn Launch in Portugal**

Inovafil, a Portuguese yarn production company, is launching a new linen yarn product and aims to promote it within the Portuguese market. The project focuses on building brand awareness, driving sales, and establishing Inovafil as a leading provider of sustainable, high-quality linen yarn. The marketing campaign will be conducted in Portuguese and will emphasize the yarn's natural origin, sustainability certifications (GOTS, OEKO-TEX, etc.), durability, and beautiful drape. The campaign will leverage online and offline channels, including a dedicated website section, social media engagement (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest), content marketing (blog posts, tutorials), public relations, partnerships with local retailers and designers, events (craft fairs), and SEO optimization. The marketing strategy aims to differentiate Inovafil from competitors by highlighting its unique selling propositions: innovative and sustainable practices, and Portuguese origin, and strong emphasis on the circular economy (InovafilRecicla).

**Target Audience Profile**

*   **Geographic:** Primarily residents of Portugal. Potential for expansion to other European markets and beyond in the future.
*   **Demographics:** Primarily focused on those who appreciate quality craftsmanship. No specific age range provided, but likely skewed towards adults and those who are interested in crafts.
*   **Psychographics:**
    *   **Values:** Sustainability, ethical production, natural materials, quality craftsmanship, environmental consciousness, and a preference for locally-sourced products.
    *   **Lifestyle:** Crafters (knitters, crocheters, weavers, etc.), designers, and brands focused on sustainable fashion or home textiles. Those who enjoy DIY projects and handmade goods.
    *   **Interests:** Eco-friendly living, DIY projects, handmade goods, high-quality materials, and knowledge of sustainability practices.
    *   **Needs:** High-quality, durable, and aesthetically pleasing linen yarn in various weights and colors. Need for information about sustainability claims and certifications.
*   **Customer Preferences:**
    *   100% linen yarn or linen blends with other natural fibers.
    *   Yarn with sustainability certifications (GOTS, OCS, OEKO-TEX) to demonstrate environmental responsibility.
    *   A variety of colors and textures, with emphasis on softness and drape.
    *   Products that are perceived as sustainable and of high quality, even if they are priced at a premium.
    *   Yarns sourced and produced locally are often favored.
*   **Engagement:**
    *   Active online presence (Website, social media).
    *   Strong engagement with social media content on platforms like Facebook, Instagram and Pinterest.
    *   Interest in educational content such as tips, patterns, and blog posts about linen yarn.
    *   Engagement with influencers, knitting/crochet shops, and designers.
    *   Willingness to participate in craft fairs, trade shows, and workshops.
